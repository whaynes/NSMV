<p>Using unit-factor method we can express a distance in meters from feet as:</p>
<p>To Find distance in meters = (distance in ft) <m>\times</m> (12 in/1 ft) <m>\times</m> (2.54 cm/1 in) <m>\times </m>(1 m/100 cm)</p>
